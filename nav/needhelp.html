Need Help?

    SAMHSA National Helpline
    A confidential, free, 24/7 information service for individuals and families facing mental and/or substance use disorders.
    National Council on Alcoholism and Drug Dependence (NCADD)
    Provides information and resources for those affected by alcoholism and drug dependence.
