    National Association for Children of Alcoholics (NACoA)
    resources and support for children affected by parental alcohol abuse.
    Alateen
    A support group for teens affected by someone else's drinking.
    National Council on Alcoholism and Drug Dependence (NCADD)
    Information and resources for those affected by alcoholism.
    Alcoholics Anonymous (AA)
    A fellowship of individuals who share their experience, strength, and hope to solve their common problem and help others recover from alcoholism.
