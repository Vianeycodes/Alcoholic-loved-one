Sip by Sip, My childhood changed

My 8 year old self was dumping bottles in the bathroom sink like it was a game. To this day I struggle to realize that my childhood was not normal.

This space is for the ones who carry stories like mine. For those who grew up in the shadow of alcohol — where childhood felt more like survival, than play.

Here, your story matters.
Here, you don’t have to carry it alone anymore.
Here you’re not alone.

Too young to fix you, Too old to help you

I was a child then, you are older now. I am living on my own building my life but I wish I could still help you.

You can write an anyomous letter here for your parent, to yourself, to your younger or future self, or a rant about whats happening and if anyone can relate. You can also just view the letters that others have written.As well as art and poetry that others have created to express their feelings.
