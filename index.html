The bottle that took my parent

Sip by Sip, My childhood changed

Before scraped knees, I was hiding bottles in the closet or dumbing it down the sink. Instead of growing up, I was forced to be the grown-up.

This space is for the ones who carry stories like mine. For those who grew up in the shadow of alcohol — where childhood felt more like survival than play.

Here, your story matters. Here, you don’t have to carry it alone anymore. You’re not alone.

Too young to fix you, Too old to help you

I was a child then, you are older now. I am living on my own building my life but I wish I could still help you.
